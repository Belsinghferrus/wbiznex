WBIZNEX - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how World Business Parliament LLC ("WBIZNEX," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you access and use the WBIZNEX platform, including the WBIZNEX mobile application (the "App") and related services. This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware and the federal laws of the United States of America.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our WBIZNEX Platform, including:

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect information such as your name, email address, password, and professional details (e.g., job title, company, industry).
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide additional information for your profile, such as your photograph, biography, and business affiliations.
  • Communications: We collect information contained in your communications with us, such as when you request support or provide feedback.
  • Transaction Information: If you make payments through our platform, we collect information necessary to process your transactions, such as payment card details.
  • Content You Post: We collect information you post, share, or transmit on the platform, such as messages, comments, and other contributions.

Information We Collect Automatically:

  • Usage Information: We collect information about your interactions with the WBIZNEX Platform, such as the pages you visit, the features you use, and the links you click.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access the platform, such as your IP address, device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Location Information: With your consent, we may collect information about your location. and preferences.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior and preferences.

Information from Third-Party Sources:

We may receive information about you from other sources, such as social media platforms (when you connect your account) and business partners.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for various purposes, including:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Platform: To operate, maintain, and improve the WBIZNEX Platform, and to provide you with the features and services you request.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: To customize your experience on the platform, such as by providing personalized content and recommendations.
  • To Communicate with You: To send you important notices, updates, and promotional materials.
  • To Facilitate Networking: To enable you to connect with other business professionals on the platform.
  • To Process Transactions:To process your payments and manage your membership.
  • To Protect Our Rights and Safety: To detect, investigate, and prevent fraud and other illegal activities, and to protect our rights and the rights of our users.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations:To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • To Analyze and Improve Our Services: To analyze how users interact with our platform and to improve our services.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with the following categories of recipients:

  • Other Members: Information in your public profile may be visible to other members of the WBIZNEX platform.
  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data hosting, and email delivery.
  • Business Partners: We may share your information with our business partners to offer you products or services that may be of interest to you.
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose your information to legal authorities if required by law or legal process, or to protect our rights and the rights of others.
  • Affiliates: We may share your information with our affiliates.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or other corporate transaction, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties with your consent.

4. Your Choices

  • Access and Correction: You may access and update your profile information through your account settings.
  • Opt-Out of Communications: You may opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the instructions in those communications or by contacting us.
  • Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings.
  • Location: You can control the collection of your location information through your device settings.
  • Do Not Track: We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with the WBIZNEX Platform and for other legitimate business purposes, such as to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country or other governmental jurisdiction, where the data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

8. Children's Privacy

The WBIZNEX Platform is not intended for children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18. If you are under 18, do not use or provide any information on this Platform. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will delete that information.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our platform and updating the effective date. Your continued use of the WBIZNEX Platform after the effective date of the revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.
